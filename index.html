Apostolic Penitentiary Decree (May 13, 2024)

Practical guidelines and theological foundations for the Jubilee indulgence.

verified Standard Conditions

  • Sacramental Confession: Sincere repentance for sins.
  • Eucharistic Communion: Full participation in the life of the Church.
  • Prayer for the Pope's Intentions: For the unity of the Church.
  • Detachment from All Sin: A firm resolution to amend one's life.

footprint Pilgrimage & Works

  • Pilgrimage: To Roman Basilicas or local Holy Doors.
  • Works of Mercy/Penance: Charitable acts or voluntary abstinence.
  • For the Homebound: Spiritual communion and prayer.
  • Special Provisions: For those in prisons or mission territories.
